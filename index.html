INSTRUCTIONS

Two players work together
to steal the treasure
before the rooster crows
and the farm family wakes!

CONTROLS

One player uses WASD
or arrows to control thief

The other player uses
mouse to move flashlight

Left click to toggle
flashlight on/off

STRATEGIC
CONSIDERATIONS

Grabbing treasure
makes a loud noise!

Flashlight can be
toggled off at any time,
but can only be toggled
on at full battery

All treasures have
unique values,
and their locations
are transient

Thief can't grab treasure
that isn't illuminated
